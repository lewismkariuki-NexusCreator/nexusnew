How is this computed? →
Trust accrual engine. Score = (completed transactions × 15 pts each, time-decayed over 60 days) + (repeat customers × 8 pts, capped at 300) + (response time bonus: ≤5min=80, ≤15min=50) + (review average bonus, after 5+ reviews) − (provider-fault disputes × 60 pts) − (no-shows × 25 pts) − (account warnings).

Tier gate. Tier requires BOTH score AND verification level. Recent provider-fault disputes (2+ in 14 days) cap you at Bronze regardless of score.
