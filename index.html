The mechanic

Built on M-PESA. Not just integrated with it.

Every provider on Nexus is a wallet. Every connection is a Daraja transaction. The entire discovery economy runs through Safaricom infrastructure — the same infrastructure 32 million Kenyans already use every day.

Clients connect for free. Providers pay to be found — starting at KSh 1 per lead. No monthly fees. No commissions on jobs. The wallet is the business model.

When the wallet hits zero, the provider disappears from the map. When they top up, they reappear instantly. The simplest retention mechanic ever built.

The Zamani Protocol

Kenya's first portable professional identity.

Every provider on Nexus builds a SHA-256 sealed, court-admissible professional identity — the Zamani Protocol. It follows them across platforms, grows with every job completed, and unlocks financial services that were never accessible before.

A mama mboga with a Bronze Zamani score of 200 has proof of 200 verified transactions. A plumber with Gold has KRA registration, GPS-confirmed location, and 147 completed jobs — all sealed and unfakeable.

At Month 12: Zamani opens a B2B data lane. Insurance companies and lenders query Zamani scores at KSh 10–20 per call to assess informal workers for products they've never been able to access. Scaled across Kenya's SACCO network, this becomes a significant revenue stream and a genuine financial inclusion engine.

Zamani score is visible on every provider profile and used for map search ranking. Higher score = higher trust = more connections.
